THE MUNCHY BRISK

+91 India Calling

located in the posh locality of JVPD scheme
The ambience is a mix of royal indian touches and allures of globalisation.
Here's what we had to relish our gluttonous appetite:
Health Sips:
+4865- Munnar ka Masala Asparagus:
Asparagus tossed with Kerala herbs and served with spiced piripiri crouton. The asparagus gave the crunch and the Kerala masala was humble. A good kickstart to the meals that were coming.
Nibbles
+11: Edamame & Ra-Ta-Lu Tokri Chaat:
Warm Sweet Potato , young Soya beans and pomegranate with sweet and sour cheese curds (ricotta).The mix is so delightful that each bite had a good balanced out taste.
Tandoor & Tawa Veg
+194: Saffron and Suran Galouti:
Nailed it. Completely. I bow to the creator of this dish. Suran or Yam as refered is used in the base mixed with the core spices and given a touch of saffron. #MustOrder
+522: Portobello Mushroom Galouti:
Italian mushroom pate stuffed with goat cheese. The mushroom here is so negligible that you will never realise it if not mentioned in the menu. Liked it but Yam galouti stood out.
+522: Yoghurt and Cheese Ke Kebab:
Hung Curd with ground spices and chives.Their version of dahi ke kebab; perfect for those who just don't want to indulge in any kind of spice. The balance of not keeping yoghurt was appreciated.
+1334- Tulsi Lifafa Paneer:
Basil Pesto Marinated Cottage Cheese wrapped in Banana Leaf.
+172-Peeli Mirch Paneer Tikka:
Lucknowi Yellow Chilli marinated cottage cheese stuffed with sweet mango pickle. Another star dish of the day. Sweet spicy variation. #MustOrder
Mains
+522- Zucchini Mussallam:
Green Zucchini stuffed with Paneer Bhurji Gravy.
+183- Langar Wali Kaali Daal:
Whole Black Lentil cooked in White butter overnight for about 6 hours. This daal is mouthwatering and just to take a shot, it's highly recommended.
Alcoholic Sips
+2366- Jambhul Karvand Martini:
Vengurla Special, Bengal currant with Bombay Saphire Gin and shaken with Plum Java Pulp and Juice.
+542- Martini in Benaras:
Traditional Benarasi Pan Muddled with Vodka.This is absolutely the best ever Martini till date.
Heavenly Desserts +22-Gajar Halwa Modak:
Carrot, Green Cardamon & Pistachio Mousse. Looks moreover like a Modak but is a melt in mouth mousse.
+4865- Hazelnut Chocolate Praline:
Belgium Chocolate Mousse with Raspberry Sorbet. It has a velvety Belgium chocolate praline sided by a Raspberry Sorbet and a Chocolate Hardshelled Cake.
+91, is an expensive affair but surely a place to indulge into the beauty of India's variety and special handpicked food treasurey.
