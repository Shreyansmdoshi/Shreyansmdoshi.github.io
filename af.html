THE MUNCHY BRISK

Almond Flowerr

As I walk along the lane strewn with molasses of residences
My eye glances across a place which spoke of elegance
A big space small enough to miss the eye
Had changed a dampen terrace erstwhile
Some days later I was invited to review this restaurant
I agreed but prayed inside "Hope the food doesn't make me it to taunt"
The Mocktails made an appearance
Bartender ensure, the good old' classics (Mojitos- Virgin and Strawberry) had their inheritance
Neither of them was sweet
Strawberry pieces gave my taste buds a good treat!
Cocktails somehow were a letdown
Kacha Aam Daiquiri was the only crown.
Rajma Galouti is the in-house speciality
The chef made the spices work in neutrality
Soft at its core, flavourful at every bite
You cannot let go this appetizer out of your sight.
Peshawari Gobi was roasted to perfection
My glutton wanted to hug it all night and day in lieu of affection
Maggi is made here in a kadai with a twist
How could I have missed?
Drenched in a strong garam masala reminding me of kadai
The tongue felt it was too hi-fi
Main course were kept simple We didn't wanted to create any ripple
Aloo Sailkoti is a rendition of scooped potatoes with spinach stuffing
We all were left huffing and puffing
Desserts consisted of Espresso Mousse
It turned out to be Lord Zeus
Matka Kulfi ruled the table with its creaminess
Our tasting ended with a breeze of happiness
