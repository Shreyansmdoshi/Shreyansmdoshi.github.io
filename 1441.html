1441 Pizzeria

1441 Pizzeria has been expanding and how. The first in the city to introduce the authentic Naples Pizza. At every bite of the pizza, you feel ease and comfort, something which you will not get at another pizza brand.
The ingredients are top notch. Special mentions to the dough they use: its airy and gets crispy fast. The centre is left untouched with its pillowy like softness and the edges gives you smoky texture of a wood fire oven.
1441 have also introduced some appetizers and beverages. We were nonchalantly surprised by how good Masala Lemonande was (I avoid lemonandes in an outlet but theirs was very refreshing and worth all the shenanigans). Crumb fried Mushrooms were astoundingly crispy and goes well with the dips. Do order their Peri Peri wings if you're a meat eater.
Smoky Dough Balls are their signature and should be ordered. Fluffly and airy with a tad crispy structure, the wood fire eminates a unique aroma and brush of butter makes it melt in your mouth.
Pizzas: (all MYO)
4 cheese:
The base was Mama Sauce, which leans towards a paprika spiked sauce. Less tangy in nature. Topped it with a blend of mozzarella, cheddar, buffalo mozzarella and smoked mozzerlla along with green bell, garlic and sundried tomatoes. You ought to go for this combination. The cheeses lends the dough and mouth a very smooth bite and garlic lowers the blandness from the cheese.
All veggie pizza:
Chose their original blend of sauce which leans towards a slight tangy taste. Topped with mozzarella, olives, paneer, jalapenos , peppers. The edges were baked cripsy and smoky and the pizza base did all the magic.
1441 also serve some ready desserts and I wouldn't recommend it at all. The Tiramisu is too creamy and lacks the coffee liquer as required. Chocolate Mousse on the other hand just has compounded chocolate. Both of them are heavily priced (Rs. 245).
A whiff of Italy in their food and a touch of India is what makes 1441 my top favourite places in Mumbai to consumer pizza!
