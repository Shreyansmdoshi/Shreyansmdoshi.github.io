Bar Stories

As I escalate down the floor,
A drop down poster with an exciting offer roared
Tempted, I made a dash for it
My legs made the fastest sprint
A big space small enough to miss the eye
Had changed an empty block erstwhile
Mocktails started to make an apperance
All of them had the perfect recipe to adherance
The classic Mojito was given a special twist
My tongue could not be more than happy with this Pineapple tryst!
Cocktails were stirred with a bit of melancholy
Only the drink 'Lost Stories' could make me jolly
Paneer Tikka was their in-house speciality
The chef made the spices work in neutrality
Soft at its core, flavourful at every bite
You cannot let go this appetizer out of your sight!
Nachos were done to perfection
I really wanted to hug it the entire night in lieu of affection!
Pizza was again made with a twist
first bite and it was out of my list!
The layer of cheese was in excess
Sriracha couldn't be tasted. did the chef just created a mess?
Main course was kept modest
The mix bag flavours of Thai Curry spoke the loudest
I was so wishing to swim in the curry
The amount of veggies made me worry
Desserts were dignified
I had a spoon which could glied
Chocolate fondant stole the show
My host for the night, @Sheena Thadani waas left with a dull expression of "Oh No!"
Gajar Halwa pleased us like never before
I am visiting Bar Stories soon, I swore!
