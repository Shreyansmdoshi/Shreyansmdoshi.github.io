Khaas Parathas

We have always heard that never judge a book by it's cover , similar stands true for a restaurant.
Never a judge a restaurant by it's ambiance.
This is a small restaurant located in the middle of a residential as well as semi commercial area.
It has very limited seating available but seems to be people's favourite spot to order from.
What we had:
Welcome Shots-
To beat this summer Heat.
Cheese Corn Stick-
Was slight dry on the inner part but crispy enough.
Cheesy Jalapeno Poppers-
a perfect one. Rare to find such finely stuffed poppers.
Paratha Basket-
Consist of 4 different varieties of Paratha's served with Raita, Black Daal and Chhole.
Pav Bhaji-
The regular people's all time favorite.
Khaas Special Falooda-
It was the regular Falooda with just Mango ice cream. Quantity was actually Khaas.
The taste for each dish served was upto the mark, VFM and quality is maintained too.
Also noted that people choose to order a lot from this place as there were continuous online orders buzzing the system and the delivery ones lining up the queue to collect their right orders. Keep it up guys.
