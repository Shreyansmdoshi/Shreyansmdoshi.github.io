The Lazy Goose

The name of this restaurant perfectly syncs with the environment. Nestled under the Nerul-Candolim beach ansd situated just beside the river, on weekdays you can see trawlers fishing and on weekend you can spend hours reminiscing life and enjoying what can easily be translated as 'Good slice of life'. Goa doesn't boast of impressive bartenders but The Lazy goose, coming from a renownded hospitality group in Mumbai, has a good bartennder and he will make sure your cocktails are highly potent.
The food:
Its an upscale gourmet food with a mix of Goan, Indian and other global dishes.
Presentation is neat and a near spectacle to the eye. Each flavours of dishes is intricated down to the detail, meaning no scope is left unturned to return the dish back to the kitchen.
Amongst the best in desserts was the Chocolate Fondast and Hazelnut Cheesecake. Both stupendifying our visit to the restaurant.
Overall, The Lazy Goose is your MUST VISIT restaurant in Goa. Your end to trip will immensely be spent along the shore. Can you expect more?
