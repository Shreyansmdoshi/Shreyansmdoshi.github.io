Geo Bistro

It's not always that a restaurant swoons me over with its near perfect food and hospitality. Arguably, my best experience of the month and infact, months to come happened at this upscale casual dining bistro.
Quick fact: A bistro is a small restaurant often serving moderately priced meals.
The owner, who are husband wife along with the consultant chef have thankfully adhered to the true definition of a bistro. Geo Bistro has almost 30 covers and serves a range of global cuisine and beverages. It also has some vegan items.
Drinks
1. Pomegranate Caprioska
2. Basil Mojito
3. Cucumber Jalapeno Martini
4. Halloween Blue Pineapple
Food:
1. Korean Cheesy Nachos:
Spinach flavoured nacho chips which is layered with gochujang cheese sauce, peppers, onions, shaved parmesan. Salsa and cheese sauce (in addition) served on side. Gochujang is a Korean red chilli paste. The sauce was lip smacking. nachos had some intriguing flavours all which was complemented by the sauce and peppers equally. And the shaved parmesan just added the fine touch and a level of sharpness.
2. Saffron Nachos:
They had mentioned saffron (kesar) in the cheese sauce. Unfortunately, when it arrived, I just felt it was regular cheesy nachos. No taste of saffron and overall, the cheese sauce felt salty.
3. Romesco baby potatoes:
Baked baby potatoes which are scooped and stuffed with a spicy tangy mix of veggies. Bite sized and goes well with drinks. The flavours here are not as intricate but manage to impresss you with its size and presentation.
4. Casado:
Costa Rican inspired meal.The original meal consists of rice, black beans, plantains, salad, tortilla and a protein. Geo Bistro has each element except black beans have been replaced by paprika spiked kidney beans and a tomato sauce is given for maintaining the moisture of dish. Opted for grilled paneer. Neat. A perfect meal for two.
5. Locro de zopallo:
Peru inspired dish. Stew made of pumpkin, potato, green peas, onion, garlic, yellow pepper, cheese, cilantro, corn and served with butter parsley rice and baby potatoes. Let me get this straight~ this dish is bland and mostly not suited for Indian palates. It was authentic, no wonder but as a customer, lets avoid ordering it since we won't like it.
Dessert:
Nutella Pretzel Shake:
Salted pretzels, nutella, milk, whipped cream and chocolate ganache. When you can drink your dessert, why wait? Simply order this. It's as appealing to taste buds as it is to eyes.
Overall, Geo Bistro is a restaurant which carves a wand over your doomed mood with its inspiring food and light ambience.
P.S: I was invited for a tasting and no part of this review has been influenced by the management.

My plan for the day is to indulge in this plate of Saffron Nachos and watch TV . . Saffron Nachos at GeoBistro, Viman Nagar. Although the menu mentioned a offbeat combination of saffron and cheese, upon tasting there was not the definitive flavour of saffron but only the sharp hue.
